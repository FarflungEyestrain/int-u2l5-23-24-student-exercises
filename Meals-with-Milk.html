Meals with Milk

#1. Cereal

Cereal is a popular breakfast food typically made from processed grains such as wheat, oats, corn, or rice. These grains are often toasted or puffed and can be sweetened or flavored before being packaged. Cereal is usually enjoyed with milk and sometimes topped with fruits or nuts. It's known for its convenience, variety, and nutritional value, often fortified with vitamins and minerals to enhance its health benefits. Milk is often added to enhance it's delicious flavor.

#2. Cookies

Cookies are a beloved baked treat, typically small, flat, and sweet. Made from a dough that includes flour, sugar, butter, and eggs, cookies come in a wide variety of flavors and textures. They can be soft and chewy or crisp and crunchy, and often include additional ingredients like chocolate chips, nuts, or dried fruits. Cookies are enjoyed worldwide and are a staple of home baking and commercial production, cherished for their delightful taste and versatility. Milk is usually added as a refreshing beverage.
